Investing in Cryptocurrency


Which Cryptocurrency?

In order to invest in cryptocurrency, you have to choose certain currencies, or crypto coins, that you want to invest your money. There are several factors to think of when trying to find the best coin to make money off such as:
  • The Community- The large part of crypto's value and success is of course it's community, so make sure the crypto of your choice as a huge following of people that truly believe in the cryptocurrency. To make sure of this, it's best to look through subreddits, youtube videos, and forums.
  • The Team- Researching the team behind specific cryptocurrencies that you might want to invest in is key as it's important to understand who is behind something you could invest a lot of money in. Look for their track record(successful or not), are they trustworthy, etc.
  • White Paper- Every new cryptocurrency comes with a report/guide known as a white paper that outlines important features of the coin such as it's purpose, technology, and it's vision, so it's always good to go through it and see whether the currency has potential to be something big.
  • Pricing History- In order to predict the rise and falls of a cryptocurrency in the future, it's beneficial to look through the history of the coin's prices. It'll allow you to understand the volatility of the currency and see if the coin is resilient when the market crashes. If it's at it's highest value, it's best to wait for the price to drop a bit and then invest. Live Prices

    How to Invest?

    The first step to invest in crypto is setting up your own digital wallet, where all transactions and digital money is stored in. This can be done through different platforms such as blockchain.com, coinbase.com, and more. The platforms where you create your wallet are the same place used as the exchange centre for all cryptcurrencies, and once you've created your wallet, and linked it to your bank account, you can start investing in all the different crypto coins you want.
