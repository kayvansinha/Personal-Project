The Value of Cryptocurrency


Supply & Demand

The demand of crypto increases and decreases based of how useful these currencies get. If more and more business start accepting crypto coins, or if the crypo's system further improve their transaction speeds, the demand for cryptocurrencies will definitely increase. The supply of cryptocurrency however is a bit less predictable as it depends on crypto mining, which is how many new coins are being mined, as well as how many crypto owners are wanting to sell their coins.


So, How Does it Work?

The value of cryptocurrencies rises, and falls, based on the markets insight of it's value, and these 'fluctuations' are established through the supply and demand factors talked about above. The main concept of supply and demand is if the demand is higher than the supply, the value rises. When cryptocurrency gets more efficient, and useful, people want to own more coins, driving the demand higher, and since these people want to use these coins, they don't want to sell them, which means cryptocurrencies demand is higher than it's supply, and is exactly how the value of cryptocurrency works.
