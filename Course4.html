Crypto Mining


What is Crypto Mining?

Buying and selling cryptocurrency on exchange platforms isn't the only way to invest and make money from crypto. This is because of cryptomining, a process in which highly advanced computers and softwares solve cryptographic equations, sort of like complex puzzles. Solving "complex puzzles" like these validates and adds transaction records to the blockchain, and as a reward, miners gain cryptocurrency. To simplify, computers solve math problems that feed data to the currencies blockchain and earn cryptocurrency from doing so. While this might not be the easiest system to pursue, in the long term, it is a great way to make money from cryptocurrency and serves as a significant factor to many crypto owners and investors.
